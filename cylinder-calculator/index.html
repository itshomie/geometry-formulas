Direct answer

What does the cylinder calculator solve?

The cylinder calculator solves volume, base area, lateral surface area, total surface area, radius, and height. It uses formulas including V=πr2h, LSA=2πrh, TSA=2πr(r+h), and reverse formulas for height or radius.

Cylinder Calculator FAQ

What inputs does the cylinder calculator need?

It needs radius and height.

Does it calculate total surface area?

Yes. It uses TSA=2πr(r+h).

Does it calculate lateral surface area?

Yes. It uses LSA=2πrh.
