This Privacy Policy explains how Blue Core Technologies LLC handles information for Geometry Formulas.

Last updated

Who we are

Geometry Formulas is operated by Blue Core Technologies LLC. You can contact us through the contact page.

Information we may collect

When you visit the website, standard technical information may be processed automatically, such as browser type, device information, pages viewed, approximate location derived from IP address, referring pages, and interaction data. If you email us, we receive the information you choose to send.

Cookies, analytics, and advertising

The website may use cookies or similar technologies for site functionality, analytics, performance measurement, and advertising. If Google ads are enabled, Google and its partners may use cookies or identifiers to serve ads, limit repeated ads, measure ad performance, and help show more relevant advertising where permitted.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. You can also learn about Google advertising controls through Google’s ad settings and privacy tools.

How information is used

  • To operate and improve the website.
  • To understand which formulas, calculators, and pages are useful.
  • To respond to messages sent by email.
  • To protect the website from abuse, spam, or technical problems.
  • To support advertising, measurement, and compliance when ads are used.

Third-party services

We may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, security, or advertising. These services may process information according to their own policies. The website is designed as a public educational resource and does not require user accounts.

Children’s privacy

Geometry Formulas is an educational website, but it is not designed to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has sent personal information to us, use the contact page so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, use the contact page.
