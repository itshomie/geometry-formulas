About Geometry Formulas

Geometry Formulas is a student-focused reference site for common 2D and 3D geometry formulas, visual diagrams, examples, and calculators.

What we publish

We publish geometry formula sheets, calculator pages, SVG diagrams, step-by-step examples, and practice problems for students who need quick homework checks and clearer formula explanations.

Editorial approach

Each core formula page is built around the same structure: a direct answer, a formula card, a labeled diagram, a calculator, examples, common mistakes, practice problems, and related pages. Formula content is written as text and rendered with KaTeX so it remains readable and accessible.
