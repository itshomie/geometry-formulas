Direct answer

What does the triangle calculator solve?

The triangle calculator solves area, perimeter, missing base or height, and right-triangle hypotenuse. It uses formulas such as A=1/2bh, P=a+b+c, and a2+b2=c2 with step-by-step work. It keeps area units and side-length units separate.

FAQ

Triangle Calculator FAQ

What inputs do I need for triangle area?

You need the base and the perpendicular height.

What inputs do I need for triangle perimeter?

You need all three side lengths.

Can it check a right triangle?

Yes. Enter side a and side b to calculate the hypotenuse using the Pythagorean theorem.
